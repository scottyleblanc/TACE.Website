# Websavers Account Review

Checklist to complete before Stage 3 decisions can be finalized.
Log into Websavers and capture the following details.

---

## Billing

- [ ] What is the exact renewal date (month and year)?
- [ ] What is the renewal cost?
- [ ] What does the renewal cover — is it one line item, or separate charges for hosting, domain registration, and email?
- [ ] Is domain registration billed separately from web hosting?

---

## Domain

- [ ] Confirm `tacedata.ca` is registered through Websavers (they are the registrar, not just the host)
- [ ] Is there a transfer lock / registrar lock on the domain?

---

## Email

- [ ] What email address(es) exist under `tacedata.ca`?
- [ ] Is email hosted on Websavers, or forwarded somewhere else?
- [ ] Is there anything in the inbox that matters, or is it effectively unused?

---

## Hosting

- [ ] What hosting plan is active (shared hosting, WordPress-specific, etc.)?
- [ ] Are any other sites or services tied to the same Websavers account?
